About Us | Company Profile


In January 1988, Suero General Hospital (SGH) opened its doors to outpatients at Brgy. Rizal, Cabugao, Ilocos Sur, under the management of the owners and physician couple, Dr. Diocaesar S. Suero and Dr. Chita Segui-Suero. Two years later, in 1990, it upgraded its facilities to a five-bed capacity.

On September 18, 1994, construction began at its present location, Manila North Road, Brgy. Baclig, Cabugao, Ilocos Sur, and was inaugurated as a twelve-bed primary hospital on April 16, 1995. By September 18, 1996, with the second floor admitting patients, SUERO CLINIC/HOSPITAL became a 25-bed secondary hospital.

Construction began on the Southern wing in March 2005 and was inaugurated on September 18, 2005, with a 34-bed capacity.

Presently, another building is under construction, which started in May 2023 and is expected to be completed in 2024. SUERO GENERAL HOSPITAL is expanding its bed capacity and services, including Ultrasound, Heart Station, Eye Clinic, and more. It will also host specialized visiting physicians to cater to more patients.

As one of Ilocos Sur's advanced healthcare institutions, SGH is guided by the value of integrity and excellence that have served as the hospital's foundation since its establishment. The hospital's unwavering dedication to patient-centric care is evident in its comprehensive range of services, tailored to meet the diverse needs of the community it serves.

SGH takes pride in its holistic approach to health, emphasizing not just treatment but also education and preventive care. Through community outreach programs and health education initiatives, the hospital strives to promote wellness and empower individuals to take charge of their health.

The unwavering dedication of SGH's staff, coupled with a steadfast commitment to excellence, continues to reinforce its position as a beacon of healthcare in Ilocos Sur, earning the trust and gratitude of the community it serves.

VISION

SUERO GENERAL HOSPITAL envisions itself as a key institution fully participating in the preservation of a healthy community. Our vision extends to becoming a leading force in healthcare, striving for excellence in medical services and community well-being. We aim to be recognized as a symbol of health and wellness, contributing significantly to the overall prosperity of Cabugao and nearby towns. Our commitment involves continuous improvement, innovation, and a proactive role in addressing the health needs of the community. Together, we envision a future where Suero General Hospital stands as a trusted partner in promoting and safeguarding the health of the community we proudly serve.


MISSION

Suero General Hospital is dedicated to delivering excellent, professional, and quality hospital care for the people of Cabugao and nearby towns. We maintain a firm commitment as a partner of the government in alleviating poverty by providing affordable medical services and implementing Philhealth insurance. Additionally, we strive to promote relevant and realistic management to ensure the integrity of medical progression.
