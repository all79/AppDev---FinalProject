Company Profile

about us

Suero General Hospital

is a leading healthcare institution committed to providing high-quality, compassionate, and comprehensive medical services. Established in 1988, our hospital has consistently upheld a reputation for excellence in patient care, medical research, and education. We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated medical professionals, and a patient-centric approach.

Our History

1988

Inception

January 1988: opens its doors to outpatients at Brgy. Rizal, Cabugao, Ilocos Sur.

1994

Relocation and Expansion

Construction begins at the present location, Manila North Road, Brgy. Baclig, Cabugao.

2005

Southern Wing Expansion

March 2005: Construction begins on the southern wing.

September 18, 2005: Inauguration with a 34-bed capacity.

1990

Facility Upgrade

SGH upgrades its facilities to a five-bed capacity.

1996

Relocation and Expansion

With the second floor admitting patients, SGH becomes a 25-bed secondary hospital.

2023

Ongoing Expansion

SGH is expanding bed capacity and services, including ultrasound, heart station, eye clinic, and more.

Our Quest: Your Health At Its Best

Whether You Seek A Consultation, Surgery, Or A Family Check-Up, We Extend An Invitation To Experience Our Unique Brand Of Care. At Our Institution, We Pride Ourselves On Delivering This Distinctive Approach To Healthcare, Characterized By Personalized Attention, Expertise, And A Commitment To Your Well-Being.

Vision

Our Vision Extends To Becoming A Leading Force In Healthcare, Striving For Excellence In Medical Services And Community Well-Being. We Aim To Be Recognized As A Symbol Of Health And Wellness, Contributing Significantly To The Overall Prosperity Of Cabugao And Nearby Towns. Our Commitment Involves Continuous Improvement, Innovation, And A Proactive Role In Addressing The Health Needs Of The Community. Together, We Envision A Future Where Suero General Hospital Stands As A Trusted Partner In Promoting And Safeguarding The Health Of The Community We Proudly Serve.

Mission

Suero General Hospital Is Dedicated To Delivering Excellent, Professional, And Quality Hospital Care For The People Of Cabugao And Nearby Towns. We Maintain A Firm Commitment As A Partner Of The Government In Alleviating Poverty By Providing Affordable Medical Services And Implementing Philhealth Insurance. Additionally, We Strive To Promote Relevant And Realistic Management To Ensure The Integrity Of Medical Progression.
