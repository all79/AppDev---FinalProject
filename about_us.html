About Us | Company Profile


Suero General Hospital (SGH) was established on April 1988, located at National Highway, Cabugao, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It is considered as a medium-sized hospital with over 80 employees. + no. of doctors and nurses etc.

As one of Ilocos Sur's advanced healthcare institutions, SGH is guided by the value of integrity and excellence that have served as the hospital's foudation since its establishment. The hospital's unwavering dedication to patient-centric care is evident in its comprehensive range of services, tailored to meet the diverse needs of the community it serves

SGH takes pride in its holistic approach to health, emphasizing not just treatment but also education and preventive care. Through community outreach programs and health education initiatives, the hospital strives to promote wellness and empower individuals to take charge of their health.

The unwavering dedication of SGH's staff, coupled with a steadfast commitment to excellence, continues to reinforce its position as a beacon of healthcare in Ilocos Sur, earning the trust and gratitude of the community it serves.

Vision

SUERO GENERAL HOSPITAL visualizes an institution fully participating in the preservation of a healthy community

Mission

Fully aware of its role in the community, it tasks itself to:
1. Deliver excellent, professional, and quality hospital care for the people of Cabugao and nearby towns.
2. Maintain its firm commitment as a partner of the government in alleviating poverty by providing affordable medical services and implementing Philhealth insurance.
3. Promote relevant and realistic management to ensure the integrity of medical progression.
